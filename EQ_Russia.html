Huge Earthquake Causes Tsunami

A very strong earthquake with a magnitude of 8.8 happened near Russia. This earthquake created a big wave called a tsunami. The tsunami hit Russia, Japan, and the USA.

In Russia, some buildings were damaged by waves up to 5 meters high. Luckily, no one was hurt.

Impact in Japan and the USA

In Japan, the waves were smaller, about 1.3 meters. The government told almost 2 million people to evacuate. One person died in a car accident during the evacuation.

In the USA, the state of Hawaii had waves of 1.7 meters. There was no major damage. Other countries also received tsunami warnings.

Massive Quake Triggers Pacific-Wide Tsunami

A colossal magnitude 8.8 earthquake has struck off Russia's Kamchatka Peninsula, generating a destructive tsunami that impacted nations across the Pacific. The quake, the 6th most powerful in recorded history, had its epicenter in the volatile 'Ring of Fire'.

Russia faced the most direct impact, with waves reaching 5 meters, causing significant infrastructural damage. However, due to robust building codes and an efficient warning system, Russia reported no casualties.

Global Response and Aftermath

Japan issued widespread evacuation orders for nearly two million residents. While wave damage was minimal and nuclear facilities remained secure, a tragic fatality occurred during an evacuation.

The tsunami also reached the United States, with Hawaii experiencing 1.7-meter waves, leading to temporary airport closures. Tsunami alerts were issued as far away as South America, highlighting the vast reach of the event.

คู่มือการสอนและกิจกรรม / Teacher & Student Guide

อุ่นเครื่องก่อนอ่าน / Pre-Reading Warm-Up

  • คุณเคยได้ยินเรื่อง 'วงแหวนแห่งไฟ' (Ring of Fire) หรือไม่? มันคืออะไร?
    Have you ever heard of the 'Ring of Fire'? What is it?
    Answer Guide: The Ring of Fire is a major area in the basin of the Pacific Ocean where many earthquakes and volcanic eruptions occur. It's a long, horseshoe-shaped path where the Earth's tectonic plates meet, making it a very geologically active region.

คำถามทดสอบความเข้าใจ / Comprehension Questions

สำหรับระดับพื้นฐาน (For Basic Level)

  • อะไรเป็นสาเหตุที่ทำให้เกิดสึนามิในข่าวนี้?
    What caused the tsunami in this news?
    Answer: A very strong magnitude 8.8 earthquake near Russia caused the tsunami.
  • ประเทศไหนที่โดนคลื่นสึนามิสูงที่สุด และสูงเท่าไหร่?
    Which country was hit by the tallest tsunami wave, and how high was it?
    Answer: Russia was hit by the tallest wave, which was up to 5 meters high.

สำหรับระดับสูง (For Advanced Level)

  • ข่าวบอกว่ารัสเซียไม่มีผู้เสียชีวิต ทั้งที่อยู่ใกล้ศูนย์กลางแผ่นดินไหวมากที่สุด เพราะเหตุใด?
    The news says Russia had no casualties despite being closest to the epicenter. Why?
    Answer: Russia reported no casualties because of two main reasons mentioned in the text: 1) They have robust (strong) building codes, so their buildings did not collapse easily. 2) They have an efficient warning system, which allowed people to get to safety in time.

หัวข้อสำหรับอภิปราย / Discussion Topics

  • ระบบเตือนภัยที่ดีมีความสำคัญอย่างไรในการรับมือภัยธรรมชาติ?
    How important is a good warning system in dealing with natural disasters?
    Guiding Points for Discussion: A good warning system is critically important because it saves lives. It gives people precious time to move to higher ground, secure their property, and prepare. It turns a potential catastrophe into a manageable emergency. Students can discuss the difference between knowing a disaster is coming and being caught by surprise.
