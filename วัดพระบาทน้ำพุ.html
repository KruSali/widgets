Wat Phra Bat Nam Phu, a famous temple in Thailand, is in the news for a big problem. Its main monk, Alongkot, and a famous medium named 'Mor Bee' were arrested. They are accused of taking money that people donated to the temple.

Wat Phra Bat Nam Phu, a prominent temple in Thailand, has become the center of a major scandal. Its abbot, Alongkot Poolmuk, and a well-known spiritual medium have been arrested on charges of embezzling donations.

The temple built its reputation over three decades as a sanctuary for AIDS patients, a cause that garnered immense public sympathy. This compassionate image was instrumental in attracting billions of baht in contributions. Authorities are now investigating if these funds were misappropriated.
