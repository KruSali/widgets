10 อันดับสายงานราชการที่เป็นที่ต้องการสูงสุด

The Thai government has many job openings. In 2024, there are over 34,000 empty positions. The government needs many kinds of workers. The jobs are separated into two groups: the Academic Category and the General Category. For the Academic group, the most needed jobs are in healthcare, like Professional Nurse, Public Health Technical Officer, and Physician. For the General group, they need many office workers like General Administration Officer and support staff in healthcare. This shows that Thailand needs more people to work in government, especially in public health services.

Recent data from Thailand's Office of the Civil Service Commission reveals a significant number of vacancies within the public sector for the 2024 fiscal year, totaling over 34,000 positions. The report categorizes these openings into two main types: the Academic Category and the General Category. The healthcare sector shows the most urgent demand in the Academic Category, with top vacancies for Professional Nurse, Public Health Technical Officer, and Physician. In the General Category, administrative and technical support roles are most needed, such as General Administration Officer and Public Health Officer. This data highlights a critical need for manpower, particularly in roles essential to national public health and administration.
