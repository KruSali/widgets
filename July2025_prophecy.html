คำทำนายภัยพิบัติ 5 ก.ค. 2025 เปลี่ยนไปแล้ว?

เรื่องย่อ (ฉบับเข้าใจง่าย)

A Japanese manga artist, Ryo Tatsuki, made a famous prediction.
She said a great disaster would happen on July 5, 2025.
Many people were worried because she had predicted big events correctly before.
The book about her prediction sold over a million copies.
An airline even reduced flights to Japan because tourists were scared.
But now, she has changed her story a little.
She says something might not happen on that exact date.
However, government experts say it's impossible to predict earthquakes and that the information is false.

Summary (Advanced Version)

Japanese manga artist Ryo Tatsuki, known for accurately predicting past events, now revises her stance on a controversial prophecy.
She previously claimed a great disaster would strike Japan on July 5, 2025.
This prediction, published in her reissued manga, sold over a million copies and caused a stir across Asia, even leading one airline to reduce its flights.
Recently, Tatsuki has backed away from the specific date, stating something will not "necessarily" happen on July 5.
She still maintains, however, that this July will be an important turning point for the region.
The Japan Meteorological Administration has strongly refuted her claims, stating it is scientifically impossible to predict earthquakes and calling such predictions false information.
